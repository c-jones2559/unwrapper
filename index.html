Start by downloading your Extended Streaming History from this site.
It says this may take up to 30 days, but it only took 2 for me and I have over 5 years of listening data.
Once Spotify emails you your data, put the .json files in the field below and you're sorted!
All data processing is done client-side which means it all happens on your computer and I never see it, making it super secure!
